Our Story

From a Classroom
Idea to a Movement

The Consulting Club of Lagos was born in 2019 out of a simple question: why are Lagos students not winning national case competitions? A small group of final-year students decided to change that. They started meeting weekly, cracking cases, sharing frameworks, and inviting industry professionals to speak.

Today, CCL has grown into a 500+ member community with alumni at McKinsey, BCG, Bain, KPMG, PwC, and leading tech and finance firms across the continent. Our bootcamps, case competitions, and mentorship programmes have redefined what it means to be a business student in Lagos.

2019
CCL founded by 8 students at the University of Lagos
2020
First case competition — 3rd place nationally
2021
Launched mentorship programme with 15 industry mentors
2022
Reached 200 members; first MBB placement
2023
Won national case competition; 500+ members
2025
Expanding to 3 partner universities across Lagos
